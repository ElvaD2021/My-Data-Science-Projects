This looks like literacy and life expectancy are positively related to each other. In other words, in countries with higher literacy rates, theres also a longer life expectancy.

But! Really, weve lumped together female and male data points with each country which isnt really the right way to treat this data. Lets use facet_wrap() to make this one plot into two separate plots.
